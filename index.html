Notes & Options

  • Harbor View Park is a short uphill walk from the station. Great Bay Bridge views from the rose garden and observation deck
  • Little shade between Osanbashi, Red Brick, and the ferris wheel — bring water
  • Cup Noodles Museum: last entry 17:00, closed Tuesdays. Adults ¥500, high school students and under free. My Cupnoodles Factory (+¥500) takes ~45 min, so it's doable if you enter by 16:00
  • Ferris wheel: ~15 min per ride, ¥1,000 (Sat 11:00–20:00). Great views even before sunset in summer
  • Running behind? Skip Osanbashi Pier to buy back time for the second half
